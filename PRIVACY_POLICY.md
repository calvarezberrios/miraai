# Privacy Policy for Mira

_Last updated: June 11, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the Mira Discord bot ("Mira", "the Bot", "we", "us") handles information when you interact with it on Discord. Mira is an AI conversational / virtual-character ("VTuber") bot operated by Carlos Alvarez Berrios, aka GameRaiderX ("the Operator"). By adding Mira to a server or interacting with it, you agree to this Policy.

## 1. Who operates Mira
Mira is a personal, independently operated bot run by Carlos Alvarez Berrios, aka GameRaiderX. It is not affiliated with, sponsored by, or endorsed by Discord Inc. Contact: carlos.alvarezberrios@gmail.com, Discord: gameraiderx.

## 2. Information we process
When Mira is present in a channel (text or voice) and configured to read it, it may process:

- **Message content** — the text of messages sent in channels Mira can read, including any text you direct at it.
- **Identifiers** — your Discord user ID, username, and server/display name, plus the server and channel IDs where messages occur.
- **Voice audio** — if Mira joins a voice channel, participants' audio is captured and converted to text (transcribed) so Mira can understand and respond. Voice features may not be enabled in every server.

Mira does not request your email, payment details, or any account information beyond what Discord exposes through normal bot operation.

## 3. How we use information
Information is used solely to operate the Bot:

- to read messages/audio and generate conversational responses;
- to maintain short-term context within a conversation; and
- to build a long-term "memory" of facts and conversation summaries so Mira can refer back to prior interactions.

## 4. Local processing — no third-party AI clouds
Mira's language understanding, speech recognition, and speech generation run **locally on the Operator's own hardware**. Your messages and voice are **not** sent to third-party AI providers (such as OpenAI, Anthropic, or Google) for processing. Note that, as with any Discord bot, your data necessarily passes through Discord's platform to reach Mira; Discord's own Privacy Policy governs that transmission.

## 5. Data storage and retention
To give Mira memory, derived information — such as conversation summaries and individual facts you mention (for example, a stated preference) — is stored in a local database on the Operator's machine.

- **Raw voice audio is not stored.** Only transcribed text may be incorporated into context or memory.
- Stored information is retained until it is deleted by the Operator, removed by routine maintenance, or deleted at your request (see Section 7).

## 6. Data sharing
We do **not** sell, rent, or trade your information, and we do **not** share it with third parties for advertising. Information may be disclosed only where required by law or to protect the rights, safety, or property of the Operator or others.

## 7. Your choices and rights
- **Stop collection:** stop interacting with Mira, or ask a server administrator to remove the Bot from the server.
- **Deletion:** you may request deletion of stored information associated with you by contacting carlos.alvarezberrios@gmail.com, Discord: gameraiderx. The Operator is able to erase the Bot's stored memory.
- Depending on where you live, you may have additional rights (such as access, correction, or deletion) under laws like the GDPR or CCPA. Contact us to exercise them.

## 8. Children's privacy
Mira is not directed to children. You must meet Discord's minimum age requirement (at least 13, or older where your local law requires) to use Discord and to interact with Mira. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under the applicable minimum age; if you believe a child has provided information, contact us and we will remove it.

## 9. Content and tone
Mira is a fictional character whose personality includes playful, flirtatious banter. It is intended for users who meet the minimum age requirement and are comfortable with that tone. Mira is not intended to produce explicit sexual content.

## 10. Security
The Operator takes reasonable steps to protect stored information, which is kept locally rather than on public infrastructure. However, no method of storage or transmission is completely secure, and Mira is a personal project provided without guarantees.
